Scissor Work

Haircuts using shears to create a variety of styles.

Razor Work

Haircuts using a razor instead of or in addition to shears to create more textured looks.

Clipper Work

Haircuts using clippers to cut hair short and close and fade between lengths.

Foils

Foil highlights are used to create customized haircolor such as sunkissed streaks or bold contrasts.

Balayage

The word "balayage" is French and means "sweeping". It is a highlighting technique that involves free hand painting lightener onto the hair.

Single Process Color

A hair coloring technique that applies a single shade to the entire head in one step.

Fashion Color

A process using direct dyes to create customized vivid, multi-colored hair.
